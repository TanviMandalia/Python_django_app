Quick Health Tips

Simple tips from Dr. Dhvani for a healthier life

Walk 30 Minutes Daily

Regular walking strengthens joints, improves circulation and keeps your musculoskeletal system healthy.

Stay Hydrated

Drinking 8 glasses of water daily helps muscles recover faster and keeps joints lubricated.

Sleep on Right Mattress

A medium-firm mattress supports your spine properly and prevents back pain during sleep.

Ergonomic Workspace

Set up your desk at the right height to prevent neck and shoulder pain from prolonged sitting.
